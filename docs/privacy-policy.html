Privacy Policy for Brewy

Last Updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Brewy ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

Brewy requests access to your device's camera to allow you to take photos of your coffee brewing process. This feature is optional and you can choose not to grant camera access. When you use the camera feature:

  • Photos are stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to our servers
  • Photos are only used within the app for your personal brewing records

Local Data Storage

Brewy stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your brewing profiles and preferences
  • Recipe information and brewing history
  • App settings and configurations

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Brewy app functionality
  • Store your brewing profiles and recipes locally
  • Improve the app's features and user experience

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or transmit personal data to external servers
  • Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to take a photo

Third-Party Services

Brewy does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Brewy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
